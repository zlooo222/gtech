VMGO

Vision

To be a premier digital learning hub that develops competent, creative, and innovative ICT learners in illustration, drawing tools, and animation.

Mission

The mission of GTECH is to provide quality and innovative learning resources that integrate technology and creativity, fostering excellence in ICT education.

Goals

I – Innovation

We embrace new ideas and digital tools to make ICT learning creative, engaging, and future-ready.


C – Creativity

We value imagination and artistic expression, encouraging learners to explore illustration, drawing tools, and animation with originality


T – Transformation

We commit to transforming learning experiences by connecting technology with education, empowering learners to grow in both skill and character.

Objectives

1. Create infographic materials to explain the functionality and applications of different drawing and animation tools.

2. Organize content visually to emphasize step-by-step procedures and tool usage.

3. Integrate interactive elements (such as clickable infographics and animations) to increase engagement.

4. Evaluate the effectiveness of graphical materials using student feedback and performance improvement.

5. Encourage creative learning by encouraging pupils to use infographic-based information in their own projects.
