LESSONS

PRELIM

It introduced to the principles of visual media and design throughout this period. It gives students the fundamental knowledge and abilities to produce and modify visual content and emphasis on developing a solid foundation in digital creation and comprehending how visual components successfully convey ideas.

MIDTERM

Students develop their abilities in dynamic media at this stage. In order to prepare students for more complex multimedia production, the objective is to help them comprehend how audio and motion enhance digital projects.
FINALS

This phase emphasizes technical expertise, creativity, and the capacity to create polished multimedia productions appropriate for practical uses.
