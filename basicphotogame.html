📸 Basic Photography



📖 Game Instructions

Mechanics:

  • Enter your name and click Play to start.
  • The game contains 7 questions, each showing one image.
  • Choose the correct photography concept based on the image.
  • You have 10 seconds to answer each question.
  • The game will automatically proceed after answering or when time runs out.
  • Use Next, Back, or Quit to control the game.
  • After finishing, click View Score to see your results.
  • Your score will be saved in the Leaderboard.

Rules:

  • Only one answer is allowed per question.
  • Unanswered questions will be marked as “No Answer.”
  • Skipped questions will not earn points.
  • Final score is based on correct answers out of 7.
  • Try to get the highest score and rank on the leaderboard.
